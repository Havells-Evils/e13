Chef spotlight

Élodie Marchand

"Cooking is memory — I want each plate to feel like a familiar yet surprising story."

After training at Le Bernardin and Noma, chef Élodie returned to her roots to open Savoir. Her style combines precise technique with deep respect for terroir. She personally forages herbs and works with small farmers to bring out the purest flavors.
